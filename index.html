About Me

My name is Zach Wilson, I am 28 years old, born and raised in South County, RI. I have been doing this kind of work for about 7 years now, and have always enjoyed working with my hands, fixing things, and helping people in general.
Starting at a young age I learned many skills from my father, who was a contractor for many years, and even more through experience and figuring things out on my own. I have no fear of being presented with a tricky situation or a task I haven't done before, as I have a knack for figuring things out, and such situations add to my experience. However, if I have any reason to believe a particular task is so above my skillset that I can't complete it flawlessly and safely, I will always be upfront and honest about it.
I have lots of regular clients whom I have helped with various projects, repairs, and property maintenance for years now, and all are more than happy with my work. I am very well equipped with quality tools and experienced at using them.

I have always had a special appreciation for the artful character and quality of old homes, as well as antique furniture, ever since I was a child. In recent years that has evolved into a passion, and currently my specialty is shifting in that direction. I wanted to learn how to do the things that so many people either don't consider can be done anymore or simply don't bother, so i did. Today, everything is manufactured to be disposable, so it's important to me to help fix and save things from a better time which were made to last. Since mastering wood window glazing and restoration, I've become more skilled at woodwork, which allows me to specialize in services that i am really passionate about.

I pride myself on being respectful, trusted, honest, and reasonable. I genuinely enjoy helping people, and it is extremely important to me that clients are 100% satisfied with my work.
